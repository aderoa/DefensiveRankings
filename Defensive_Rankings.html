Methodology — How the Defensive Score Works

Archetype Detection

Players are classified on a perimeter↔interior spectrum using a sigmoid on BLK/G. High-block bigs get interior weights; guards/wings get perimeter weights. This determines which stats matter most for each player.

Interior Formula (weights sum to 100)

DEF FG% DIFF (22) · BLK/G (22) · DEF_RATING (14) · NET_RATING (10) · DEF FG% (10) · CONTESTED/G (8) · STL/G (5) · DEFL/G (5) · REB/G (4)

Perimeter Formula (weights sum to 100)

STL/G (22) · DEFL/G (16) · DEF FG% DIFF (12) · PF/G (8) · NET_RATING (8) · WIN (8) · CHARGES/G (6) · DEF FG% (6) · DEF_RATING (6) · MPG (4) · LOOSE BALLS/G (4)

Anchor Bonus (up to +18 pts)

Players on top-15 defensive teams (by real team DRTG) earn a bonus scaled by: team rank tier (top-5/10/15), minutes share (25–34+ MPG), scheme factor (player DRTG vs team DRTG), and win factor (vs median).

Normalization

All stats are z-scored within the current season pool. The final composite is 0–100 normalized where 100 = best defender in the league.
